SAINT (Saving Animals In Need Today) began in 2000 when our founder began rescuing and adopting out cats and kittens, personally paying all veterinary and medical expenses. The needs became so great, SAINT was formally incorporated in 2010 and became a nonprofit 501c3 animal welfare organization.

In October 2013, the Cat Adoption Center in Anamosa was destroyed by a fire. SAINT relocated to SW Cedar Rapids and held our grand opening in March 2014. We are able to accommodate more cats in the Cedar Rapids Adoption Center, averaging 70+ cats.
