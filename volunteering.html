SAINT is run and staffed solely by volunteers. No one in our organization receives compensation for the work they do. The majority of our volunteers have families and work full-time jobs in adddition to their volunteer duties.

Opportunities

When you volunteer at SAINT, you make a difference for homeless cats.

Cleaners

Each day we have two cleaning shifts, AM and PM. Volunteers are responsible for cleaning the cages and community rooms. This includes sweeping, refilling dry food, providing fresh water, and scooping litter boxes.

Open House Greeters

SAINT is open every Saturday from 11am-3pm. During this time, we need volunteers to greet our visitors and take them through the shelter. You may also have time to socialize and play with the cats and kittens.

Fostering

Fosters are needed for a variety of reasons. Cats or kittens may be placed with a foster for a few days; others may need a few weeks or months.

  • Caring for kittens, especially those that are newborns.
  • Socializing cats or kittens that are not completely comfortable with humans.
